About KewlKraft

KewlKraft is an OP Survival server that’s completely non pay-to-win. Everything — from crate keys to ranks — is earned through gameplay. Our focus is on fairness, safety, and building a welcoming community for everyone.

What Makes KewlKraft Unique

  • Free Crate Keys & Monthly Events: Earn new crate keys every month, with special ones for holiday events — all free.
  • Kewl Levels & Progression: Gain XP from mining, fighting mobs, and exploring. Rank up every 5 levels to unlock perks and free ranks.
  • Custom Holiday Plugins: Enjoy seasonal features built specifically for each holiday month.
  • Community-Focused: Toxicity and harassment aren’t tolerated. Everyone should feel welcome and safe.
  • Helpful Staff: Our volunteer staff team ensures fair play and a smooth server experience.

Rules & Code of Conduct

  • No griefing, stealing, or exploiting bugs.
  • No hacked clients, x-ray, or unfair modifications.
  • Be respectful in chat; casual swearing is fine, but don’t be rude.
  • No racism, discrimination, or hate speech.
  • No spamming, advertising, or self-promotion.
  • No NSFW or inappropriate content.
  • Respect staff and all members of the community.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. KewlKraft does not collect, track, or share any personal data. We don’t use cookies, analytics, or third-party tracking tools. Gameplay data (like inventory and builds) exists only within the Minecraft server environment and is not stored externally.

Terms of Service

By joining KewlKraft or participating in our Discord, you agree to follow the server’s rules and community guidelines. Staff may issue warnings, mutes, or bans if necessary to maintain a safe and fair environment. Continued use of our services implies agreement to these terms.
