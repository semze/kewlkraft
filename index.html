How donations work

We buy server credits in blocks: 1,800 credits = $10. Our goal is to secure 6 months (10,800 credits) of credits = $60. Donations cover hosting, backups, anti-cheat, and community events.

  • Minimum donation: any amount — every bit helps
  • Primary payment: Cash App — Cash Tag: $itschiping
  • We keep donors updated on Discord with major purchases

Frequently asked questions

Will donations give in-game advantages?

Donations keep the server online and pay for features. We do not sell pay-to-win advantages; any perks will be cosmetic or strictly balanced.

Is my data safe?

Cash App handles payments; we do not receive payment card details. If you send a screenshot or transaction ID, use Discord DMs for verification.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by the payment provider. Contact us on Discord if there's an issue and we'll help coordinate.

Do you publish donation usage?

Yes — big purchases and important updates are posted in the Announcements channel on Discord.
